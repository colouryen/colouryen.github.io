About Me

Chia-Cheng (Jerry) Yen

I am a second year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of California, Davis under the supervision of Professor Dipak Ghosal. Before starting my academic career at UC Davis, I completed my M.S. degree in Computer Science at the National Tsing Hua University, Hsinchu, Taiwan and worked as a research assistant at the Institute of Information Science, Academia Sinica, Taiwan. I currently work with the C3PO research group and our research topics focus on improving the traffic throughput and analyzing security threats to traffic networks under a Connected and Autonomous Vehicles environment. My research interests are Reinforcement Learning and its applications, especially on Traffic Signal Control, Decision Making, and Optimization. Outside the research, I love cooking my country dishes and baking brownies.

For more information about me: Google Scholar/ LinkedIn/ GitHub/ CV. (last updated in 02/20/2019).

Contact information: ccyen@ucdavis.edu
